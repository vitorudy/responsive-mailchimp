DAPS Global Events App

Attendee Getting Started Guide

This is official DAPS Global Summit application. You will be able to access all the information on this app before, during and after the event.

This app will enable you to :

  • View the full delegate list including photographs
  • See the most up-to-date conference programme
  • Engage in live polls during the conference sessions
  • Access pams and floor plans for the event
  • Message other attendees
  • Post questions to the speakers
  • Keep up-to-date with announcements from eventSpark
  • Participate in surveys
